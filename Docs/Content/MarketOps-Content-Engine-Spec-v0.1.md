# MarketOps Content Engine Spec v0.1

Created: 2026-05-07 13:28:20

---

## Purpose

Turn long-form MarketOps articles, reports, trade breakdowns, and development updates into reusable short-form content across multiple platforms.

---

## Inputs

- weekly blog posts
- monthly blog posts
- trade case studies
- P&L reports
- dashboard summaries
- strategy reviews
- development updates
- lessons learned
- compliance-approved notes

---

## Outputs

### X / Twitter
- short posts
- threads
- punchy market/project observations

### Instagram
- carousel copy
- reel concepts
- captions
- visual post summaries

### Facebook
- conversational post summaries
- blog-share summaries
- discussion starters

### LinkedIn
- professional thought-leadership posts
- build-in-public updates
- educational summaries

### Video
- short-form scripts
- talking points
- hook lines
- CTA lines

---

## Minimum Publishing Goal

2 posts per week per platform:

- X
- Instagram
- Facebook
- LinkedIn

---

## Standard Workflow

1. Staff Writer publishes long-form source article
2. Growth Desk extracts themes, lessons, stats, quotes, and hooks
3. Growth Desk creates per-platform post drafts
4. Video Desk creates supporting video scripts where appropriate
5. Compliance Desk reviews
6. Content enters posting queue
