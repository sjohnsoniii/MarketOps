## Purpose

This amendment adds the media, growth, social publishing, and branded avatar-presenter layer to MarketOps.

MarketOps is not only a trading lab and signal platform. It is also a public-facing content and education engine.

---

## 1. New Team Roles

### Growth Desk / Social Media Expert

Responsible for:

- transforming long-form blog posts into short-form content
- producing at least 2 posts per week for each target platform
- adapting content for X, Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn
- writing hooks, captions, and CTAs
- creating content calendars
- supporting audience growth and engagement
- repurposing development updates into public-facing posts

### Video Desk

Responsible for:

- short-form video concepts
- reels/shorts scripts
- platform-specific video hooks
- visual storytelling around MarketOps updates, trades, strategy behavior, and lessons learned
- turning long-form material into short video content

### Brand Presenter / Avatar Desk

Responsible for:

- defining and maintaining the visual identity of the MarketOps virtual presenter
- ensuring continuity of appearance, tone, and persona
- supporting recurring video content using a branded hyperrealistic avatar
- maintaining a reusable presenter spec for future media generation

---

## 2. Content Pipeline

MarketOps content should flow like this:

Trade / strategy / performance / development data
-> Staff Writer creates long-form article
-> Growth Desk repurposes into short-form platform content
-> Video Desk creates video scripts and concepts
-> Compliance Desk reviews
-> Content is published

---

## 3. Content Cadence

Minimum weekly cadence target:

- X: 2 posts per week
- Instagram: 2 posts per week
- Facebook: 2 posts per week
- LinkedIn: 2 posts per week

Optional extensions later:

- reels/shorts
- newsletter recap
- YouTube or long-form video
- dashboard-linked update posts

---

## 4. Content Intent

The purpose of the content engine is to:

- build audience
- explain MarketOps activity
- teach investing/trading concepts
- create public proof
- document wins and failures
- create reusable material for future signal product growth
- create reusable media infrastructure for future projects

## 6. Reuse Strategy

The Growth Desk, Video Desk, and Avatar Desk should be designed as reusable internal systems.

## 7. Governance

All public content generated by the media/growth pipeline must:

- maintain paper vs live honesty
- avoid misleading financial claims
- avoid guarantees
- be reviewed by Compliance Desk before publication
- stay aligned with MarketOps brand voice
- avoid degrading the presenter into explicit/adult content

Sexy is acceptable.
