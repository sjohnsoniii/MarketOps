# MarketOps Draft Publishing Checklist v0.1

Use this before manually posting any MarketOps draft.

## Safety

- Confirm the post says paper simulation, fake/sample money, or sample-data preview when discussing MarketOps outputs.
- Confirm the post says not financial advice where market research is discussed.
- Confirm there is no buy/sell/copy language.
- Confirm there are no guarantees or performance claims.
- Confirm there are no private/internal IDs.
- Confirm there are no local paths.
- Confirm there are no credentials, secrets, tokens, or account details.
- Confirm no unreviewed agent improvement proposal is described as implemented.

## Content Fit

- Confirm caption length fits the platform.
- Confirm hashtags are not misleading.
- Confirm links point to `https://sj3labs.com/marketops` or another appropriate public MarketOps page.
- Confirm video/avatar content is intentionally experimental if presented that way.
- Confirm chart screenshots show public-safe sample-data labels.

## Platform Fit

- Instagram: visual first, concise caption, clear disclaimer when needed.
- X: short, direct, no hype.
- TikTok: clear hook, simple captions, no financial instruction tone.
- YouTube Shorts: strong title, visible paper/sample framing, no overclaiming.

## Final Gate

Manual posting only. No auto-posting is enabled.
