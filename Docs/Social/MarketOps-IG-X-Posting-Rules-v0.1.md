# MarketOps IG / X Posting Rules v0.1

## Scope

Instagram and X are the first platforms for manual MarketOps publishing experiments. This document defines review rules only. It does not enable posting, scheduling, API access, secrets, or account automation.

## Required Framing

- MarketOps is in development.
- MarketOps is paper simulation / fake-money / sample-data preview only.
- MarketOps content is educational market research, not financial advice.
- MarketOps does not provide live execution, portfolio management, brokerage connection, or subscriber trading.

## Approved Language

Use language like:

- paper signal
- simulation
- lab note
- development log
- paper dashboard
- research observation
- risk-first market research

## Prohibited Copy Direction

- Do not tell anyone to enter or exit a position.
- Do not suggest copying MarketOps activity.
- Do not imply guaranteed outcomes.
- Do not imply real-money performance.
- Do not imply live trading.
- Do not imply a paid signal or subscription product exists.
- Do not expose internal IDs, private notes, local paths, secrets, or account details.

## Link Rule

Use `sj3labs.com/marketops` only after a human confirms the specific draft is approved for manual posting. A link in a draft does not authorize posting.

## Approval Rule

YES approval means approved for later manual copy/paste review only. It does not mean post now, schedule now, send now, connect an API, or publish automatically.
